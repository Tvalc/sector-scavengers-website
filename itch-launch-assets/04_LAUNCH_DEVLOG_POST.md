START

The demo is open.

You get the full salvage loop: three cards in hand, an energy meter, a hull that does not forgive, and the corporate clock counting down every third raid. Soft cap of three hours per session so you can taste the loop without the full campaign yet.

Things to try in your first run:

- Push past the "extract" prompt at least once. See what the wreck is hiding.
- Declare nothing. Watch the smuggled stack do work.
- Lose on purpose. Void XP only grows when you stop running scared.

Found a bug or a moment that hit harder than it should have? Comment below with browser and OS. I read all of them.
